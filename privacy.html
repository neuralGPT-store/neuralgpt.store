Privacy

We collect only what we need to run the marketplace. This page explains how we use information you provide when contacting us or applying as a vendor.

Data usage

Information submitted via contact forms or onboarding forms is used to evaluate commercial opportunities and to communicate updates. We store contact details and messages to manage deals and partnerships. We do not sell personal data.

Contact forms disclaimer

Messages submitted through our contact forms are handled manually by the partnerships team. By submitting a message you consent to be contacted by our team. We retain submissions for the purpose of evaluating and progressing commercial discussions.

Payments and personal data

At present, no payments are processed on the platform. When we activate payments, we will update this policy with specific details about payment data handling and processors. Until then, any payment-related details are collected only after explicit manual agreement in the commercial approval process.
