What is NeuralGPT.Store

NeuralGPT.Store is a curated marketplace for AI hardware, sensors, and embedded solutions. We connect manufacturers and specialised providers with enterprise buyers, integrators and developers seeking quality hardware and reliable suppliers.

Benefits for Providers

  • Access to an engaged technical and enterprise audience.
  • Integrated product listings and analytics dashboard (vendor portal).
  • Secure payments and standard invoicing workflows.
  • Priority for verified and high-quality suppliers.

Commission Model

Comisión de lanzamiento reducida al 10 %. Nuestro modelo de comisiones es sencillo: una tarifa fija sobre ventas completadas. Aplicable a Standard, Pro y Enterprise durante el periodo de lanzamiento. Condiciones especiales para proveedores early.
